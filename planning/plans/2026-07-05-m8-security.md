## Task 1 ‚Äî Lesson (analogies + ‚â•1 Mermaid)
Analogy: shipping an AI system without SBOM/scan/sign is **shipping food with no ingredients label, no
health inspection, and no tamper seal**. Cover: **sandboxing** agent/tool/generated-code execution in
ephemeral isolated containers (gVisor, ToolHive per-server isolation); the supply chain ‚Äî **Syft** (SBOM)
‚Üí **Grype/Trivy** (scan) ‚Üí **Cosign** (sign & verify) for model AND agent images; hardening (least
privilege, read-only rootfs, drop caps, no-new-privileges, health checks, secrets); **guardrails &
evaluation** (input/output guardrails ‚Äî reuse M6's; lightweight evals + tracing); **governance without a
vendor** (policy on what agents may reach, which creds/MCP tools). Mermaid: source ‚Üí build ‚Üí SBOM ‚Üí scan
(gate) ‚Üí sign ‚Üí registry ‚Üí verify-on-deploy. ~1100‚Äì1400 words.

## Task 2 ‚Äî Lab (uses `labs/m8`)

correctly), then the **GitHub Actions** `security-pipeline.yml` (build‚ÜíSBOM‚Üíscan-gate‚Üísign). Plain
`docker`/CLI in prose. Troubleshooting: cosign registry auth (write:packages / --allow-http-registry),
scanner DB download, trivy exit-code gating.
